I had the feeling that I was being influenced by the number of views a video had. For example, when watching a comedy video, I saw that it didn't have a lot of views and I A) felt like the video should have more views and B) questioned whether the video was "really" that good. To prevent this influence, I decided to create a chrome extension that hid everything.

Link: https://github.com/jarett-lee/statistics-hider
